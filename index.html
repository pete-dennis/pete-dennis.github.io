Bio

I am a musician, artist, and programmer. Given the rise of the fascist technocratic oligarchy I am choosing to reclaim some level of control over online prescence. Many artists feel trapped into social media spaces as a way to let their friends know about their art. I'm deciding to make my attempt at breaking this paradigm by creating a website where I can live stream music, link to my bandcamp, and talk with my friends. Yes, all online communications with me will be directed through this website and the email attached to it. Below are my upcoming live dates.
